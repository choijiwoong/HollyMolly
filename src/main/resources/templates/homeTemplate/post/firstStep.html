1. What is Osirau service?

Osirau service provide matching syetem across disable people and volunteers.
You can join as a user traveling, or can join as a guide showing the surrounding area. Disable anyosne who want to take a walk or travel some area can be matched guide who are in your place or beautiful place where you want to go in osirau. Available anyone who want to help someone near your place can do your activity as a guide with recognition of service.

2. How does it work?

Using kakao map api service, we can make request for travel or volunteer.
guide or user can show their location on map. Then, guide can receive requests that disable people send to you and, user can send request to guide on map. After connectting each other, you can make meeting plan in our safty service without you professional information.
